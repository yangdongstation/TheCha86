Don't read x86 as eks-eighty-six anymore!

It's cha-eighty-six.

  • "cha" is the pronunciation of "cross" in Chinese, and "X" looks like a cross.
  • If you pronouce it as "cross-86", we're the same.

This website aims at spread of the "cha" pronunciation of "x".

Please star the repository and share it with your friends and workmates if you agree with us!
